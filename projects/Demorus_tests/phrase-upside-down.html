Your ARPU is a measure of a customer’s average contribution to revenue. A rising level means you’re getting more sales from each customer and you have pricing power. Of course, that’s just a starting point. An average can’t tell you anything about the quality of your sales. We all know that some customers are more valuable than others! You need to get really granular and look at how sales break down by channel and customer type so you can tease out trends and devote resources to optimizing your customer mix and boosting your share of wallet.
